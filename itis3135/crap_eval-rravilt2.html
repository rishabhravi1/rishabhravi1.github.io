Website Evaluations

Apple

The official Apple website showcases their latest products and services with sleek and modern design.

  • Contrast: Excellent use of bold text and high-quality imagery for clarity and appeal.
  • Repetition: Consistent fonts, colors, and button styles across all pages.
  • Alignment: Everything is perfectly aligned, creating a clean and organized look.
  • Proximity: Related elements are grouped logically to improve usability and navigation.

CRAPPY Webpage

A deliberately poorly designed website to illustrate bad design practices and what to avoid.

  • Contrast: Poor contrast makes text hard to read and strains the eyes.
  • Repetition: Lack of any consistent style or visual patterns.
  • Alignment: Misaligned elements create visual chaos and confusion.
  • Proximity: Scattered elements make it hard to group related information.
