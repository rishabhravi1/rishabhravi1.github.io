Project Overview

Purpose

The web application promotes Technogenz Solutions by showcasing their IT and data consulting expertise, streamlining client communication, and displaying case studies.

Intended Users

  • Business Owners and Decision-Makers
  • IT Professionals
  • General Audience

Content Overview

  • Home Page: Overview of services and a hero section
  • About Us: Company vision and leadership
  • Services: Detailed consulting offerings
  • Case Studies: Success stories
  • Contact Us: Interactive form

Wireframe and Site Map

Dynamic Functionality

The website will include interactive features such as a contact form with validation, an image gallery, and expandable service descriptions.
